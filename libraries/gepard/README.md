## `gepard`

### What is this library?

This library is a third-party Python library that is used by nuclear physicists who wish to come up with models for CFFs and GPDs and compare them to experiments or make predictions for future experiments.

### What is the resource we're following?

We are following the documentation at this link: https://gepard.phy.hr/

## Notes:

1. `gepard` has a *super convenient class* that contains almost *all* of the experimental DVCS data there is. It eliminates the need for the self-extraction of data, i.e. going through paper after paper and manually typing into `.csv` files. Link: https://gepard.phy.hr/datasets.html

### Terminology:

1. **CFF** - Compton Form Factor
2. **GPD** - Generalized Parton Distribution (function)
3. **DVCS** - Deeply-virtual Compton scattering
